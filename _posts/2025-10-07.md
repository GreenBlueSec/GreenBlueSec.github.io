### 📖 Introduction

**"Spooky Season is Cybersecurity Season**
Thus there is no better time to go on a **"Cyber Phantoms Hunt"**!

The internet is a cool and creepy place where you can find both great joy and great spook... Sometimes in the same material. One of those chaotic characteristics is the ability for individuals from almost anywhere on our planet to connect to each other. Whether done through direct connection or through multiple exchanges of information. A downside of this connectability is the opportunity it provides for criminals and nefarious individuals to scam people and your communities. That is why we are embarking on a **"Cyber Phantoms Hunt"**. You'll be searching the web for phantom versions of yourself. We won't just be elminating these phantoms as some are friendly and useful, but we will be wrangling them no matter what.

I want to focus first on behaviors/efforts for the beginner level computer user. Meaning, to get full value of this article, you must have access to a computer with internet and basic browsing skills. If you feel that's above you, here are some great resources for brushing up on your computer skills... But, I'm be pretty curious on how you found this blog in the first place.

#### Mission

This document outlines a series of objectives to create your personal information security/risk management program and start leveling up your digital self-defense skills. We are going to start with creating an Account & Assets Inventory. Here we are setting up our digital perimeter. Take time working through all 10 items below keeping your focus on only youraccounts even if you have dependents or other family members who rely on you. First work through this list entirely on your own then worry about your families accounts.

Stay tui

Tips:
* To get the most out of this exercise, dedicate at least four hours of your time in total.
* Make list of your own to track your progress and any notes.
* Remind yourself that cybersecurity requires a bundle of small and large habits. By completing this exercise, you're already on the up.

### ✅ Account & Assets Inventory Action Items

#### Objective 1 - Prepare A Cyber Base
Before you start chasing down your phantoms, we need to prepare some tools and storage locations.

| Step | Title                                         | Procedures                                                                                                                                                     |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| A    | Set Up/Confirm Multifactor Authentication on Your Mobile Device                    | 1: Choose a multifactor mobile application. Most devices are pre-installed with a multifactor authenticator, so first check your device. Otherwise, I have used both **Google Authenticator** and **Duo Mobile** and would recommend both.                   |
| B    | Set Up Password Manager                    | 1: Choose one: **Bitwarden**, **1Password**, **Proton Pass**, etc. <br> 2: Import or enter all existing credentials. <br> 3: Enable biometric unlock on mobile.                    |
| C    | Create a Free Cloud Storage Account           | Sign up for a free service (e.g., **Google Drive**, **Proton Drive**, **Dropbox**) where you can safely store backups of important files, credentials, and documents.     |
| D    | Create an Account Inventory                     | Create a list of all the accounts you actively use in your cloud . <br> - Identify **critical accounts** tied to health, finance, cloud storage, work, etc. <br> - Confirm if 2FA/MFA methods are available and enabled.   |

#### Objective 2 - Locate, Catalog, and Erase Phantoms
Now that your cyber base is set up, you will begin the primary objective of hunting down your cyber phantoms

| A    | Delete Unused or Unnecessary Accounts         | - Close any accounts you no longer use. <br> - Back up anything important before deletion. <br> - ✨ *Bonus:* Request **data deletion** (GDPR/"Right to Be Forgotten").   |
| A    | Review & Harden Critical Account Security     | - Enable **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** everywhere. <br> - Use **strong, unique passwords** via a password manager. <br> - Check for unauthorized logins.          |                                                |
| 7    | Back Up Critical Data                         | Store backups of family photos, tax documents, passwords, or medical records. <br> Use both **cloud storage** and a **local encrypted drive** if possible.               |
| 8    | Audit Browser Extensions & Mobile Apps        | Remove unused or shady extensions and apps. <br> Check permissions — especially for access to email, files, or contacts.                                                  |
